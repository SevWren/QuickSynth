Read the article at [URL]. Summarize it the way a well-informed friend would explain it over 
coffee — clear, honest, and without condescension. Use this loose structure:

- Open with: "So basically, what happened is..." (2-3 sentences of plain-English context)
- Follow with: "The key things to know are..." (3-4 punchy bullets)
- End with: "The part that's still unclear / worth watching is..." (1-2 sentences)

Rules: 
- No bullet should exceed 20 words.
- Spell out any acronym on first use.
- If the story has a villain, victim, or winner — name them plainly without editorializing.
- Do not start any sentence with "It is important to note."
